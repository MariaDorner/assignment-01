My favourite recipe

My favourite thing to bake is cinnamon buns the recipe I use is by a Hungarian woman so enjoy my free translation from Hungarian to English!

Ingredients:

  • For the dough:
    • 375g flour
    • salt
    • 5tbsp sugar
    • 1.5 eggs
    • 1.5dl milk
    • 15g yeast
    • 60g butter
  • For the filling:
    • 3tbsp cinnamon
    • 180g sugar
    • 90g butter

Steps:

  1. First let the yeast rise in warm sugary water.
  2. Then mix all the ingredients together for the dough. Then let the dough rest for 1h at room temperature.
  3. Then we roll out the dough, brush it with butter and sprinkle it with cinnamon. After that we roll up the dough and cut it up into even pieces.Now it is ready to go in the oven and bake for 20 minutes at 180 degrees.
